About IIT Mandi

IIT Mandi is located in a small town Mandi,Himachal Pradesh in INDIA.It is located in a peaceful environment surrounded by mountains on all sides.The climate here varies from -2 to 18 degrees.College is 525 acres in size for a total of 3000 students(overall).There are 2 campuses namely north and south campus.The ratio of students to teacher ratio is 1:9.As it's a sceond-generation IIT so some buildings are under construction.But still,college is worth for it's location.The current NIRF ranking is 20.
