About Josaa

Josaa stands for joint Seat Allocation Authority.Josaa is a national online platform where it recieves a number of options from the students for a particular college and course and selects the topmost choice(more priority) and based on rank(hard part). It is a platform from which students are sent to iit's,nit's and other famous universities.The student is given 3 choices float,slide and freeze.Freeze basically is to lock your choices and slide is to aim for different branch in a same institute and float is for aiming different branch in a different college.It consits of 7 main rounds and 2 mock rounds.
